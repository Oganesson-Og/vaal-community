“It is with heaviest of hearts that I stand before the women and girls of South Africa this evening to talk about another pandemic that is raging in our country – the killing of women and children by the men of our country”

A grave pandemic

  • At least 1 in 4 women have experienced sexual or physical abuse in their lifetime.
  • Almost half of women report having experienced emotional or economic abuse at the hands of their intimate partner.

    Fighting for the next generation

  • Children are seriously impacted by gender based violence. This can lead to intergenerational trauma and other long term problems.

    • What we do...

    Contribute to a reduction of gender based violence and to promote the rights of those subject to gender based violence; mainly women and girls. We also empower our community with knowledge of causes and expressions of gender based violence as a preventative measure of this scourge.

    • Casework
    • Community Work

    Casework

    Counselling services to survivors and perpetrators of gender based violence. Part of the support offered to victims include directing them to relevant stakeholders for issues ranging from medical to legal support.

    Community Work

  • Prevention programs: awareness campaigns and community self-help projects.
  • Group work: parental skills training to men and boys.
